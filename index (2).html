About

VIRINCHI-2K26 is a National Level Cultural Festival organized by the School of Engineering and Technology, Sri Padmavati Mahila Visvavidyalayam. The festival aims to provide a platform for students to showcase their talents in various cultural and academical activities, including music, dance, drama, and art. It also serves as an opportunity for students to interact with peers from different colleges and universities, fostering a spirit of camaraderie and cultural exchange.

Computer Science and Engineering

1 VISTARA Vistara is all about to express the views regarding a particular topic by some research. You have to put all your views and thoughts regarding to your topic on to a piece of paper designed and you need to present that using your communication skills.
2 HUMBLE BRAG Humble Brag is a website design event. In this event one should design a website based on constant time limit and given tools
3 CODE CHARADES Code Charades is a game of pantomimes: you have to “act out” a code without speaking, while the other members of your team try to guess what the code is.
